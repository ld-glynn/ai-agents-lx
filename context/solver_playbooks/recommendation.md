# Playbook: Recommendation Document

## Purpose
Write a clear, actionable recommendation for a decision-maker.

## Structure

### 1. Problem Summary (2-3 sentences)
What pattern of problems does this address? Reference specific problem IDs.

### 2. Recommendation (1 paragraph)
What should we do? Be specific and direct.

### 3. Evidence
- Which problems support this? (cite IDs)
- What pattern was identified? (cite pattern ID)
- What's the confidence level?

### 4. Expected Impact
What will improve, and how will we measure it?

### 5. Effort & Timeline
How much work is this? Who needs to be involved?

### 6. Risks & Alternatives
What could go wrong? What else did we consider?

### 7. Next Steps
Concrete actions with owners. Maximum 5 items.

## Tone
- Direct, not hedging
- Evidence-based, not opinion-based
- Respectful of the decision-maker's time — keep it under 500 words
